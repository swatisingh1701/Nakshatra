What is the Universe?

The universe is all of space and time and their contents, including planets, stars, galaxies, and all other forms of matter and energy. It's estimated to be about 13.8 billion years old.

🚀

Why Humans Explore Space?

Humans explore space to understand our origins, search for new resources, find potential habitats for humanity, and answer fundamental questions about our existence and the nature of the cosmos.
